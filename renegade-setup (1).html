Windows — What Happens Next

Your download has started. Once complete:

  1. Find RenegadeSolutions-Connect.exe in your Downloads folder
  2. Double-click it
  3. Click Yes when Windows asks for permission
  4. Setup runs silently and closes on its own ✅
Takes 3–5 minutes. If you see a blue SmartScreen warning, click More info then Run anyway.
🍎

macOS — What Happens Next

Your download has started. Once complete:

  1. Find RenegadeSolutions-Mac-Connect.command in your Downloads folder
  2. Double-click the file — a terminal window opens automatically
  3. Enter your Mac password when the prompt appears — it won't show as you type, then press Enter
  4. The window runs and closes on its own when complete ✅
Takes 3–5 minutes. Do not close the window until it shows SUCCESS.
